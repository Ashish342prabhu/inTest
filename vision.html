Vision & Mission

Towards a better future...

Vision:

“To be the role model of learning, research, innovation and sustainability for the knowledge society.”

Our vision extends to nurturing a new breed of high-tech entrepreneurs through incubation activities, while also actively engaging with existing MSME units in the region. We invite innovators to join hands and shape the future through their ideas, transforming SIIC into a dynamic startup powerhouse.

Mission:

SIIC is supporting to fulfill careers for students, young researchers and scholars to their aspirations and abilities, whilw also fosteringg impactful research dedicated to addressing societal challenges and promoting poistive change.

Our mission is.

  • To promote innovation and startup through the pioneering use of technology.
  • To develop superior talent through partnerships with industry and society.
  • To promote research, discovery and entrepreneurship through collaborative action.
  • To build responsible citizens of the world by instilling a culture of seamlessness in all facets of life.
