WHY INCUBATION CENTER

Centre for Innovation, Incubation & Linkages C-BRIDGE (Center for Business, Research, Incubation, Development, Growth and Entrepreneurship) at SIIC is set up to promote innovative startups through the University ecosystem. Centre would facilitate and hone ideas and innovations that are useful to the society and address some of the problems faced through development of product, process and services.
