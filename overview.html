Who are we..?

Transforming Visions into Ventures: Igniting Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Embarking on a transformative journey, Srinivas University is set to elevate its entrepreneurial landscape with the cutting-edge Srinivas Innovation & Incubation Centre [SIIC]. Accredited under MSME and Section 8 of the Companies Act, this hub is a beacon for fostering creativity and business acumen. Nestled in the coastal districts of Karnataka, SIIC pledges to provide comprehensive business development services, propelling the growth of Micro, Small, and Medium Sector Enterprises

Fueling Futures, Sparking Creativity: Igniting Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Transforming Ideas into Impactful Products.

SIIC is not just an incubation centre; it's a catalyst for change. By infusing managerial expertise at every stage of business development, we ensure the sustainable growth of high-tech enterprises in the state.
